About

Taylor Swift is an American popstar; Mostly known for her songs written about ex-boyfriends

Her fans are called 'Swifties' and they are one of the largest fanbases for this genre of music


Taylor Swift was born December 13, 1989 in West Reading, Pennsylvania. She was a fan of music and songwriting in her early years which paved the way to her stardom. Taylor Swift rose to fame by opening for the country band, 'Rascal Flatts' at age 16.
